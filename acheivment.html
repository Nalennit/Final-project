The Defeat of Yone

Back when i was starting to get to know the game(League of Legends), there was a certain character you could play who was extremely low-skill-required and cosidered the strongest most broken character at that time. Meaning that if you were playing against an enemy player who was Yone, you were in for a hopelessly gruesome 30+ minutes of getting killed over and over as he gets stronger and you get weaker. But eventually i beat him, that in it of itself to me, is an acheivment. This was also during the time when "Lethal Tempo", a very strong sort of amplifier for the character's stats was still existent in the game.
